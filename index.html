🏫 About DIU

Daffodil International University, established 24 January 2002, is Bangladesh's largest private university campus at 360 acres in Daffodil Smart City, Savar. With 21,000+ students, 650 faculty, 23 departments and QS rank 1001–1200, DIU is a leading research and innovation hub.
